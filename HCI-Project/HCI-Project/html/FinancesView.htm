Reports

Reports window shows reported for specified Route and its schedule.

First, you can choose Route.

Also, you can leave both of these fields blank, and then a report for all routes and schedules will be generated.

To remove the selected route, press the Clear button.

The same goes for selecting and clearing the schedule.

Then you can choose if you want to choose a time period as a month, or as a specified date.

When you have specified all the attributes, press the Get Report button.

If you have chosen Months you will see the Report for the Month period, with sections as seen below.

If you had chosen a specific Date, the only difference will be that the Graph will be showing a comparison with dates of each month, up to four months ago.
