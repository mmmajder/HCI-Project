Timetable

This window lets you browse for routes that connect your desired destinations.

First, you need to choose your 'From' station.

Then, choose your 'To' station.

At last, choose your travel date.

When you have set the above parameters press the bottom button.

If there are routes conecting this two stations, you will see a window similar to this.

Select one of the table rows, and upon pressing the Buy or Reserve button you will be able to Select Seats for your ticket.
