Edit Train Route

First, you have following attributes to edit: train type and stations which will define the route.

Train type

You can choose to change current train type with one of the options from given train types.
Chosen train will be going through this route

Route station

To add new station on the route, select one stations from right list(All Stations) and drag it over and dorp to the left list (Route station).
You can also change the order of already added stations in Route station list. To remove station from the route, select one stations from left list(Route stations) and drag it over and dorp to the right list (All Stations).
Route must contain at least 2 stations, for it to be considered route.

Save/Cancel changes

If you changed your mind and no longer want to edit this route, click on "Cancel" button.
If you are happy with your inputs, click "Save changes" button to complete changes.

Edit Station

Additionally, you have an option to edit stations by clicking "Edit stations" button. This will take you to another page with detailed instructions.
