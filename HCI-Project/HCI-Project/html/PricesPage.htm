Prices

This window enables you to change ticket prices.

First, you have to choose one of the possible Routes, as shown below.

Upon selecting the route you will see a table that shows its connected stations and their representative price to the next destination.

By clicking on one of the table rows you actually select the station and you can change its price.

You can also choose a station from the Station Combobox on the right.

After you have typed in the new price, just click the Change price button.

If everything is alright, you will see the message of success.

We can now see that the prices are updated.

In the top right corner, you can also see that Undo button has appeared.

Redo button also exists and they are used to Undo and Redo changes made to prices.
