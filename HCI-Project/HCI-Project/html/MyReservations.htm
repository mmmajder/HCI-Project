My Reservations

This window shows all your future reserved tickets.

In the Top part of the Window, you see three buttons.

For any of the three to work you need first to select a reserved ticket from the ticket table in the bottom half of the window.

In case you press a button without selecting the row you will see an error.

Cancel Ticket when pressed cancels the reservation.

Buy Ticket when pressed confirms the selected reservation and buys the ticket.

Preview Ticket opens a detailed preview of the selected reservation. Which is shown below.

Pressing the Close button takes you back to My reservations window, while pressing the Cancel ticket, cancels the ticket.

Pressing the Buy button confirms the reservation and buys the ticket.
